## CITI Certification Requirements

**All residents must complete by December 31 of first year**

### Required Modules:
- **GCP** — Good Clinical Practice
- **RCR** — Responsible Conduct of Research  
- **HSR** — Human Subjects Research
- **COI** — Conflict of Interest

---

## CITI Registration Steps

1. Go to [CITI Program](https://www.citiprogram.org)
2. Create account or log in
3. **Select affiliation:** Trinity Health - Michigan Region
4. Complete your profile
5. Enroll in all 4 required modules
6. Complete each course (read + pass quiz)
7. Download certificates

⏰ **Deadline:** December 31  
📋 **Affiliation:** Trinity Health - Michigan Region

## Quality Improvement (QI)

**Local process improvement for your institution**

- Implement known best practices
- Iterative PDSA cycles
- Internal system optimization
- ❌ Usually does NOT require IRB approval
- Published as "lessons learned"

---

## Scholarly Activity (SA) – Non-QI

**Original research generating new, generalizable knowledge**

- Original research studies (IRB required)
- Case presentations & case reports
- Posters at conferences
- Peer-reviewed publications
- Oral or poster presentations

📋 [QI vs. Research Determination Worksheet](https://www.saintalphonsus.org/sites/default/files/2026-01/QI-V-Research-Worksheet.pdf)
